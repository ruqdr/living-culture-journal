# Living Culture Collaborative Digital Journal

## Project Goal
To create a collaborative, multi-media digital journal exploring a specific cultural theme related to the target language (e.g., "A Culinary Tour of the French-Speaking World," "Festivals and Traditions of Latin America," "The History of Anime in Japan"). The journal will be a living document, built and curated by the students over the course of a semester, with each student contributing a chapter or article.

## Repository Structure
- `articles/` – Contains all article markdown files.
- `images/` – Contains subdirectories for each article's media assets.
- `docs/` – (Optional) Additional documentation.

## How to Contribute
1. **Propose Your Topic:** Create a new Issue with your article proposal.
2. **Get Approval:** Wait for instructor approval and assignment.
3. **Create Branch:** Create a feature branch named `feature/your-topic`.
4. **Develop Content:** Write your article in Markdown, add media to appropriate folders.
5. **Submit Pull Request:** Open a PR to merge your branch into `main`.
6. **Address Feedback:** Respond to peer and instructor review comments.
7. **Merge:** Once approved, your article will be published!
